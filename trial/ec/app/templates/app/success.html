Welcome to Hyderabad City Council

The Hyderabad City Council, your local government body, is here to serve the community and make Hyderabad a great place to live. We understand the importance of resident well-being and quality of life, which is why we offer a wide range of services to support you. Whether you need help with essential services like waste collection and financial support, or you're looking for pension , childcaresupport, or benefits, the Council is here to assist you. We're also dedicated to keeping hyderabad a clean, safe, and vibrant city, so you'll find resources for reporting issues, parks and leisure facilities, and initiatives to support the community. Our website allows you to easily access all these services and find the information you need, but you can also reach out to us directly for help. We're committed to working together to build a better hyderabad for everyone.
