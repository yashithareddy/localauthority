Child Care Support Programs

At Birmingham City Council, we understand the importance of affordable child care for families. Our child care support programs aim to provide assistance to families, especially those with low income, to ensure that children have access to quality child care services.

Child care can be a significant expense for families, and we recognize the challenges that families face in balancing work and childcare responsibilities. Our support programs are designed to alleviate the financial burden of child care and promote the well-being and development of children.

Whether you're a working parent, a single parent, or a family facing financial difficulties, we're here to help. Our child care support programs offer various services to meet the diverse needs of families:

  • Subsidized Child Care: Access financial assistance to help cover the cost of child care services, including daycare centers, preschools, and after-school programs.
  • Child Care Vouchers: Receive vouchers or subsidies to offset the expenses of licensed child care providers, allowing families to choose the care that best suits their needs.
  • Referral Services: Get connected with local child care providers, resources, and support services to find affordable and high-quality child care options.
  • Financial Counseling: Receive guidance on budgeting, managing child care expenses, and accessing available financial resources to support your family's child care needs.

Our goal is to ensure that every child has access to safe, nurturing, and developmentally appropriate child care environments. We believe that investing in child care is an investment in the future of our community.

If you're struggling with child care expenses or need assistance finding affordable child care options, don't hesitate to reach out. Our team is here to support you and your family on your child care journey.

Contact us today to learn more about our child care support programs and services.
